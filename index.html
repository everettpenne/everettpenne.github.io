About Me

Hi, thanks for visiting my website! My name is Everett Penne and I have a degree in Engineering Physics and Mathematics from the University of Wisconsin-Platteville.

Currently I am seeking professional experience working as an Engineer or Researcher. I have a particular interest in fluid modelling (especially in water and water resources). I pride myself on my ability to think flexibly and make connections across disciplines. My drive is to use my problem-solving skills to make a positive impact on the environment and my community.

Beyond my academic and professional life, I am an avid rock climber and love playing chess and generally going outside. You can frequently find me day-dreaming about thru-hiking the Appalachian Trail or hanging out at crags in the Rocky Mountains.
